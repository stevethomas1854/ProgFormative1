The Briefest of Introductions

Did you know: More than 3 billion people are now using the internet

Over the last century computers have integrated themselves into every aspect of our lives, from connecting us across the world to driving our cars, and from controlling our defense systems to organising to-do lists. There is a reason why we are currently living in the information age. However, the power and reliability we place upon machines raise some particular qustions: How dependant should we let ourselves become? Are we losing contact with the real world? Should some areas be left technologically untouched? Whilst these are all perfectly reasonable questions it is similarly undeniable that computers have benefitted people of all backgrounds across the globe. Some help with just the small everyday tasks and day-to-day living, whilst upon others we rely to make our most important decisions. Here we are to explore in to just a few of the applications of Computer Science that we believe benefit society significantly.
